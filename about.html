This site is simply a frontend for Library Genesis that's suitable for e-ink readers.
This site is officially intended for books that are out of copyright. This site does not host files, and does not condone piracy.
This site consists of static webpages, with all parsing happening on the client-side (and therefore, open-source by default)
This site is completely portable - You can save the entire website and host it on your own WSGI server.
Search results and download links are fetched by scraping the corresponding libgen pages.
